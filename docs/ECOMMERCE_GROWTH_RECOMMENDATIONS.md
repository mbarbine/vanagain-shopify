# Ecommerce Growth Recommendations

These targets assume current analytics baselines are measured in Shopify Analytics and GA4. Treat the ranges as 90-day targets after implementation, not guaranteed outcomes.

## Highest ROI Priorities

1. Fitment finder and confidence messaging
   - Target: increase product detail page conversion by 10-20%.
   - Measurement: product page conversion rate, fitment-support contact rate, return reason tags.
   - Why: customers buying vintage VW parts need certainty that the part fits their year and model.

2. Product bundles and repair kits
   - Target: increase AOV by 8-15%.
   - Measurement: AOV, bundle attach rate, items per order.
   - Why: coolant, fuel, brake, exhaust, and tune-up jobs naturally require multiple parts.

3. Back-in-stock and wishlist capture
   - Target: recover 5-10% of out-of-stock product demand.
   - Measurement: back-in-stock signup rate, notification conversion rate, recovered revenue.
   - Why: niche parts customers often wait for the exact part rather than substituting.

4. Technical content to product links
   - Target: grow non-brand organic sessions by 20-40% and article-assisted revenue by 5-12%.
   - Measurement: organic landing sessions, article click-through to collections/products, assisted conversions.
   - Why: VanAgain has useful technical authority that should move readers into parts pages.

5. Email capture and lifecycle flows
   - Target: email revenue share of 15-25% of ecommerce revenue.
   - Measurement: email signup rate, welcome flow revenue, abandoned cart revenue, repeat purchase rate.
   - Why: classic VW owners have ongoing maintenance needs and respond to useful parts/support content.

6. Collection-page conversion cleanup
   - Target: increase collection add-to-cart rate by 10-18%.
   - Measurement: collection click-through, add-to-cart rate, search/filter usage, zero-result searches.
   - Why: fast fitment and category browsing reduces support friction and decision fatigue.

## International Growth

- Configure Shopify Markets for USA, U.S. territories, Canada, Mexico, Europe, and Australia before running campaigns.
- Show clear shipping, duties, and tax expectations on cart and product pages where Shopify data supports it.
- Add country-specific shipping FAQ entries for Canada, Mexico, Europe, Australia, and U.S. territories.
- Track conversion rate by market and currency. Pause paid spend to markets where shipping quote failures exceed 3-5% of checkout sessions.

## Paid and Organic Campaign Targets

- Google Shopping/PMax: target ROAS should be set only after feed quality and conversion tracking are confirmed. Initial target: 250-400% ROAS depending on margin.
- Search ads for high-intent SKUs and repair categories: target conversion rate 3-6% and ROAS 300%+.
- SEO content: publish or refresh 4-8 technical guides per month with internal product links.
- Retargeting: target 5-12% incremental revenue from browse and cart audiences once traffic volume supports it.

## Measurement Requirements

- GA4 ecommerce events: view_item, add_to_cart, begin_checkout, purchase, search, select_item.
- Shopify Analytics review: conversion rate, AOV, sessions by source, product conversion, inventory sell-through.
- Search Console: indexed pages, rich results, query growth, crawl errors, redirects.
- Email platform: signup source, flow revenue, campaign revenue, unsubscribe rate.
- Customer support: fitment questions, shipping questions, return reasons, out-of-stock requests.
